Cisco Security Policies

Why Verify WebEx Users

- Cisco Spark uses innovative encryption for each Spark space.

- Each user is authenticated and authorized to access a Spark Space and obtain an encryption key.

- Cisco Spark must ensure you are who you say you are to get the keys to the space

How do you verify a user?

Users verify their accounts by clinking a secure link within the email they received.

Verify Accounts

Don't you want users receive an email

When you verify Cisco Spark accounts, invited users will not receive an email. No user action required. Exceptions.

  • If you don't want users receive an email, click "verify" and you will see the following options.

    Verify Domain by entering TXT record in DNS.

    Sign a Legal Agreement that gives the administrator a 3 month grace period to enter TXT record in their DNS

  • If you want users receive an email when invited, click "Receive email" and invite users from WebEx Site Administration.
