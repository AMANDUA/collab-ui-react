Verify Domains

Verify your domain and ensure the security and integrity of your organization. Users belonging to verified domains will not be sent an email to verify their email address.

Exceptions

Sign a legal agreement that allows your organization to verify your domains with a pending status. You have 3 months to add the TXT record to your DNS and prove that you have ownership of the domain.

We provide a token to add to your domain host's DNS TXT record. We confirm that you own the domain by checking for this token on te DNS server. Once the verification is completed, go to WebEx Site Administration and invite users to Cisco Spark.
